About Us

Welcome to 42 Lisboa's Pong Project!

At 42 Lisboa, we're passionate about coding, creativity, and collaboration. Our team has come together to develop an exciting full-stack application centered around the classic arcade game, Pong. With a blend of skill, dedication, and a dash of nostalgia, we're bringing the timeless joy of Pong to the digital age.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to deliver an immersive and entertaining gaming experience that pays homage to the iconic Pong game while infusing it with modern technology and design. Through our project, we aim to showcase the power of full-stack development and the endless possibilities it offers in creating interactive and engaging applications.

Our Approach

In developing our Pong project, we've embraced a comprehensive full-stack approach, utilizing a wide range of technologies and frameworks to bring our vision to life. From frontend development using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to backend implementation with Python and Django, we've leveraged cutting-edge tools and methodologies to ensure a seamless and captivating user experience.
